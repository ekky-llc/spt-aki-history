A Post Raid Replay System for Singleplayer Tarkov

A detailed replay system with heatmap overlay, review kills, lootings, positional data, and just get a general understanding of what was going on in your offline raids!

Packed with Features

Brace yourself for the ultimate overkill of raid analysis tools you probably didn’t know you needed. From replaying your "totally pro" moments to over-analyzing bot behaviors like a true raid scientist, this system gives you everything you've always wanted.

Heatmap Overlay

Visualize your movements and actions with an intuitive heatmap for detailed analysis.

Event Timeline

Replay and review key moments like kills, lootings, and interactions, all in a convenient timeline view.

Interactive Maps

Dive into detailed, multi-level maps with real-time filters and markers for ultimate clarity.

Player Tracking

Follow specific players or bots to analyze strategies and gain competitive insights.

Detailed Raid Stats

Get a comprehensive overview of raid performance, from kills to looted items and bot interactions.

SAIN Integration

If installed, view bot personality and type details for enhanced analysis.

Kill Breakdown

Dive into detailed stats about who, how, and when you took down enemies or got taken down yourself.

Loot Tracker

Review every piece of loot you snagged during a raid and flex those gains in your squad chat.

Bot Intelligence Insights

Analyze bot behavior and decision-making to understand how they outsmarted you or didn’t.

Custom Filters

Filter events like kills, looting, or both to focus on exactly what you’re looking for in each replay.

Heatmap Customization

Customize your heatmap to display specific actions like combat, movement, or looting.

Performance Metrics

Measure and evaluate raid performance with metrics like survival time, accuracy, and more.

Power User Controls

Use keyboard controls to quickly rewind, fast-forward, and slow down your raid replays.

AI Behavior Insights

Understand how AI bots behaved during the raid, including their tactics and positioning.

Internationalization

Enjoy support for multiple languages making the system accessible to a global audience.
